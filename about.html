Our Story

We met in beautiful Portugal — what started as a chance meeting quickly turned into an unforgettable weekend. From that very first trip, we knew there was something special between us.

After Portugal, we found every excuse to see each other again, meeting in different corners of Europe and the US, squeezing in long weekends, and spontaneous trips. What began as a spark turned into a story we couldn't wait to keep writing together.

In August, in sunny Tenerife, Vikas proposed overlooking a beautiful sunset. It was a calm, unforgettable moment, and it felt like the perfect way to begin this next chapter together.

Now, we can't wait to begin our next chapter and celebrate our love with all of you in Panama — bringing together our favourite people, vibrant traditions, and a whole lot of joy in one unforgettable place.
